Create Interactive Learning Objects

Spark Learning is an online community platform that provides educators and organizations online tools to create compelling interactive learning objects.

What is Spark?

Online Community Platform

Spark provides a central location for your content creators to collaborate on eLearning projects. Your users can create learning objects, rate and provide feedback to eachothers work, find and share learning objects to use in their class, post questions and answers in the private discussion board, and much more.

Learning Object Publishing Platform

Spark also provides tools for your users to create interactive learning objects. Using Spark, your users can take existing videos (including YouTube videos) and turn them into interactive videos with decision trees, quizzes, and other interactions. They can also create immersive hot-spot images, interactive course presentations, and quizzes or feedback forms that can be easily embedded in their courses or websites.

Robust social features to bring your community to life

User Points

Members gain points for each interaction on the site.

Content Ratings

Users can rate eachothers content using a 10-star system.

Add to favourites

Users can 'favourite' content on the site.

Commenting

Easy to use commenting system to enable discussion.

Discussion Board

Your community can discuss, share ideas, and collaborate on projects in the private blogging system.

Simple tagging

Simple #hashtag system to allow members to easily tag content.

Categories and Topics

Content can be organized in categories that make sense to your organization.

Statistics

Users can view how many times their content has been accessed.

Flagging and reporting

Allow members to flag content for admins and moderators.

Create interactive training and education content with ease

Interactive Videos

Turn your existing videos into interactive learning objects by adding quizzes, text & images, interactive summaries, and much more.

Presentations

Create attention-keeping presentations that can include videos, quizzes, games, and Twitter feeds.

Hot-Spot Images

Turn an image into an interactive object by placing hotspots that, when clicked, displays images, texts, and videos.

Question Sets

Create mini quizzes or surveys that you can embed in any web page or LMS.

More reasons to love Spark!

100% Open Source

Spark is built on Open Source technologies, including Drupal & H5P. We will also be releasing Spark as an open source project very shortly.

Collect Metrics

All interactions are tracked and you can easily see the results of the interactions, including quiz score.

Embed Anywhere

Create your content once, embed it anywhere - now any changes you make will automatically be shown everywhere you have embedded your content.

Responsive Design

Your content will look great on mobile and tablets right out of the box - no coding required.

Accessibility Standards Compliant

We made great stides to comply to the AODA standards - Spark and the learning objects you create are WCAG 2.0 AA compliant.

Canadians Only

Our products and services are catered to Canadian educators - We make our services and cloud products available only to Canadians. Not to mention or hosting is on 100% Canadian servers!
